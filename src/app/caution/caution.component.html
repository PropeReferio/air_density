It is crucial that you understand the following before using this app for swooping!

  1. Dry air is denser than moist air.
  2. Air density does not always increase at an even rate with an increase in altitude. It is possible to have a "normal" DA between 0-200 ft, and a much higher DA at the top and middle of your turn.
  3. High Density Altitude means less dense air.
  4. Low Density Altitude means denser air.
